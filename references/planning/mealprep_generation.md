# Meal Prep Generation

Input:
- calories and macros
- meals per day
- food preferences/exclusions
- allergies
- budget
- cooking time
- storage capacity

Build:
- daily meal structure
- grams per ingredient
- batch-cook steps
- storage notes
- swaps with similar macros

Output:
- daily calories/macros
- meal table with ingredients and grams
- prep schedule
- storage/reheating notes
- substitutions

Keep plan practical and repeatable. Do not over-optimize variety if user asked
for meal prep efficiency.
