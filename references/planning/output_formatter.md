# Output Formatter

Use concise structure:

1. Calories and macros
2. Constraints: allergies/exclusions, budget/tools, batch size
3. Meal plan table
4. Meal prep steps
5. Storage/reheating notes
6. Macro-safe swaps
7. Shopping list, if requested
8. Check-in metrics to report next week

For meal prep tables, include meal, ingredients with grams, portions, and
calories/macros per meal or per day. Keep storage/reheating notes present even
when brief.

For adaptations:
1. What changed
2. New calories/macros or meal swaps
3. Why in one short line
4. Next check-in target

Keep formatting clean. Avoid long theory sections.
